Process Explorer

May 12, 2009

If you’re looking for a bit more information than Task Manger manager provides, Process Explorer, also from Microsoft may be the answer. With it you can view your processess in a hierarchical tree, find out what processess are safe and find out which process is running a particular window. These are just a few of the capabilities, and you can configure it to run instead of Task Manager at the click of a button.
